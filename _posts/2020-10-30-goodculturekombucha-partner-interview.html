What does Good Culture Kombucha do and what is your role in the company?

We distribute the finest organic Kombucha & Water Kefir ingredients that solves producers greatest production challenges like scaling up, quality, consistency & lowering alcohol.

Over the past 2.5 years we have grown from zero customers to now having over 75+ kombucha brands using our product (Manna-K) in their production every month. We are super focused on what we do & try bring our customers the very best products, service & support. I am the proud CEO of Good Culture Kombucha.

Since when have you been following the Kombucha market and why?

I have personally been a big fan of kombucha for nearly 10 years. Before setting up Good Culture Kombucha I work for Remedy Kombucha helping them expand their business, it gave me great insight into the challenges every kombucha producers faces, how to solve them & the insights to start Good Culture Kombucha and help others succeed.

What role does your business want to play in the Kombucha market with its products?

We supply a growing 75+ kombucha producers across Europe and further afield with their Kombucha ingredients every month & play a critical trusted role in their supply chain. We solve their production challenges, offer great technical support & hopefully build long lasting relationships.
