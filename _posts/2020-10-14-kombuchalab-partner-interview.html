What does CDR KombuchaLab do and what is your role in the company?

CDR KombuchaLab is an innovative system for the analysis of Kombucha, which is used to optimise the production process obtaining results in real time.

It can be used in all production phases from the beginning of fermentation to the bottling of the finished product. With CDR KombuchaLab you can perform a complete in-house quality control obtaining real time results of analyses in order to promptly take the best decisions during the fermentation process avoiding dangerous organoleptic deviations.

The reagents are pre-filled and ready to use and it is not necessary to handle toxic or carcinogenic agents or to use laboratory tools. If you need help, the help function will lead you through the analysis process ‚ÄúStep by Step‚Äù. You can perform the tests not only in laboratory, but also at production line, even by anyone with no experience in laboratory techniques.

CDR KombuchaLab Analyses:

  • Alcohol by Volume
  • Acetic Acid
  • Fermentable Sugars
  • Gluconic Acid
  • Total Lactic Acid
  • Yeast Assimilable Nitrogen (YAN)
  • Glycerol
  • Total Polyphenols

What role does your business want to play in the Kombucha market with its products?

We want to support kombucha brewers during their production process and to check the quality of their final product. CDR KombuchaLab helps producers to improve the quality of kombucha optimising the production process with ease, speed and accuracy.
