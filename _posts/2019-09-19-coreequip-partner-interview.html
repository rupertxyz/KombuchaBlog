What does Core Equipment do and what is your role in the company?

Core Equipment is a leading equipment supplier to beverage producers around the world. With customers in the UK, USA, Europe and Asia we have vast experience of providing equipment solutions to Kombucha producers, wineries, cideries, distilleries and breweries. We offer full installation services, aftersales support and servicing, extending the life of your equipment. My role as Sales Manager enables me to meet first hand with existing and potential customers, to discuss equipment selections and ensure the customer is provided with a high level of service. I am also heavily involved with the project management side of new opportunities to help tailor the right equipment solutions for our customers and deliver the correct solution.

Since when have you been following the Kombucha market and why?

We have been actively involved in the Kombucha Market for several years and already have a number of Kombucha customers in the UK. We are also in discussions with numerous European based customers about future opportunities in the Kombucha market. Kombucha is a huge growth market worldwide making it a key focus for Core. We aim to become the preferred equipment supplier for Kombucha producers globally.

What role does your business want to play in the Kombucha market with its products?

Core Equipment represent many of the industry's leading equipment manufacturers and offer expert advice in the equipment for Kombucha brewers. We can help our Kombucha customers producers plan their equipment purchases and ultimately grow their businesses.
