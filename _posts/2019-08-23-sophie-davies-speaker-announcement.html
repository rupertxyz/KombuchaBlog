Kombucha Summit is getting closer by the day and we're confirming last speakers! Today, we are super excited to announce Sophie Davies who is a Fresh Foods Buyer at Planet Organic in London. Her talk is going to highlight Planet Organic's Kombucha journey so far and what's yet to come. We're looking forward to a talk about Kombucha from a retail perspective.

About Sophie

Sophie is a Fresh Foods Buyer with Planet Organic for four years and has closely watched the rise of the Kombucha industry.

About Planet Organic

Planet Organic is a London-based health food store, serving eight communities around the capital with a vast range of fresh organic produce, health foods for speciality diets and natural beauty products, with a focus on sustainability and the environment.
