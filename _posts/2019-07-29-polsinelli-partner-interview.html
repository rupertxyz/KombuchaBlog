What does Polsinelli do and what is your role in the company?

Polsinelli Enologia produces equipment for wine, beer and oil production and equipment for the beverage industry in general. Daniele Polsinelli is the owner and CEO of the company.

Since when have you been following the Kombucha market and why?

For 3 years now because Kombucha is a bio product that seems to be very attractive in terms of quality and future development of the market, very in line with our vision.
