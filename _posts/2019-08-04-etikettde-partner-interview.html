What does etikett.de do and what is your role in the company?

As an expert for self-adhesive and wet-glue labels, etikett.de is the ideal printing partner for your Kombucha labels. As the online market leader for custom labels, we rely on efficient technology, state-of-the-art machinery, lean workflows and a dynamic team. This guarantees you the best print quality, adherence to delivery dates, competent service and maximum satisfaction.

Since when have you been following the Kombucha market and why?

Particularly in the USA, the Kombucha tea drink is very much in vogue and can now be found on almost every beverage shelf. For the European market, too, it is now time to pick up on the trend and establish the drink in the local retail trade. Kombucha is an interesting product as a natural and refreshing alternative to often artificially tasting and excessively sweetened soft drinks. Kombucha compliments another trend in the food industry: customers who are particularly interested in a healthy diet and lifestyle rely on the fermented tea drink. As a product label manufacturer, we would like to support and promote the growth of Kombucha on the German market with unique and high-quality labels.

What role does your business want to play in the Kombucha market with its products?

True to the motto "The eye drinks too", etikett.de provides refreshingly original and high-quality labels for the perfect design of your Kombucha product. We want to help the tea drink appealing to the general public, represent its special characteristics, arouse the interest of European customers and make them true Kombucha fans.

What are your expectations in exhibiting at the Kombucha Summit?

We hope to make new contacts and learn more about the world of Kombucha. We would like to use these new insights and experiences and transform them into creative energy in order to make our products even better and to be able to adapt them precisely to the requirements and wishes of our customers. At the Kombucha Summit 2019 we would like to be inspired by interesting conversations and lectures and at the same time pass on and represent our passion for the production of unique and high-quality labels.
